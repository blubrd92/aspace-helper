Privacy Policy

Last updated: March 20, 2026

ASpace Helper ("the app," "we," "our") is a browser-based tool that helps library archivists prepare bulk import data for ArchivesSpace. This policy explains what information we collect, how we use it, and your choices.

1. Information We Collect

Account Information

When you create an account, we collect:

  • Email address — used for sign-in and account recovery
  • Display name — shown to members of your institution
  • Password — stored securely by Firebase Authentication (we never see or store your plain-text password)

If you sign in with Google, Google provides your email address and display name. We do not receive or store your Google password.

Institution and Team Data

  • Institution name — the name of your organization or team
  • Role — whether you are an admin or member within your institution
  • Invite codes — generated codes used to invite new team members

Project and Archival Data

The archival metadata you enter into projects (titles, dates, container information, etc.) is stored to enable your work. This is professional/institutional data, not personal information about you.

Automatically Collected Information

  • Timestamps — when your account was created, when you last signed in, and when projects were created or modified
  • Session data — Firebase Authentication stores a session token in your browser's local storage to keep you signed in

What We Don't Collect

We do not use analytics, advertising trackers, or third-party cookies. We do not track your browsing behavior, IP address, or device information beyond what Firebase Authentication requires to function.

2. How We Use Your Information

Your information is used solely to operate the app:

  • Authenticate your identity and maintain your session
  • Display your name to other members of your institution
  • Store and retrieve your archival project data
  • Enforce access controls (institution isolation, admin permissions)
  • Send password reset emails when you request them

3. How We Store and Protect Your Information

All data is stored in Google Firebase (Authentication and Cloud Firestore). Firebase provides:

  • Encryption in transit (TLS/HTTPS) and at rest
  • SOC 1, SOC 2, and SOC 3 compliance
  • Data centers in the United States

Firestore security rules enforce institution-level isolation — users can only access data belonging to their own institution.

4. Who Can See Your Information

  • Your institution's members can see your display name and email
  • Your institution's admins can additionally manage your role
  • Other institutions cannot see any of your data
  • We do not sell, rent, or share your information with third parties for marketing or advertising

5. Your Choices

  • Update your profile — you can change your display name, email, and password at any time from the My Profile modal
  • Leave your institution — you can leave your institution from your profile, which removes your access to that institution's data
  • Export your data — project data can be exported as CSV at any time
  • Delete your account — contact your institution's admin or the app administrator to request account deletion

6. Third-Party Services

The app uses the following third-party services:

  • Google Firebase (Authentication, Cloud Firestore) — Firebase Privacy Policy
  • Google Sign-In (optional) — Google Privacy Policy

No other third-party services receive your data.

7. Children's Privacy

ASpace Helper is designed for professional use by library and archival staff. It is not intended for use by children under 13, and we do not knowingly collect information from children.

8. Changes to This Policy

We may update this policy from time to time. The "Last updated" date at the top will reflect any changes. Continued use of the app after changes constitutes acceptance of the updated policy.
